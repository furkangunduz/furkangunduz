## About Me
I am a FullStack Developer with experience in building scalable backend systems and user-focused applications. At **Pixa Software**, I work
on e-commerce integrations and develop backend services with NestJS and GraphQL. At **RaceData.ai**, I led a team to build a telemetry
analysis platform for racing games, scaling it to over 1,000 users. Previously at **Kodgaraj**, I contributed to e-commerce projects, 3D
design modules, and mobile app conversions. I enjoy solving real-world problems and creating reliable software solutions.
